## Quick Update Methods by Platform:

---

### If You Used **Netlify** (Drag & Drop):

**Method 1: Re-upload (Easiest)**
1. Go to [https://app.netlify.com](https://app.netlify.com)
2. Log in to your account
3. Find your deployed site
4. Go to **Deploys** tab
5. Simply **drag and drop your updated `LeafyPop` folder** again
6. Wait a few seconds - your site will automatically update!

**Method 2: Using Netlify Drop (Alternative)**
- Go to [https://app.netlify.com/drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)
- Drag and drop your `LeafyPop` folder
- It will automatically detect and update your existing site

---

### If You Used **Vercel**:

1. Go to [https://vercel.com/dashboard](https://vercel.com/dashboard)
2. Log in
3. Find your project
4. Click on it
5. Go to **Deployments** tab
6. Click **"Redeploy"** button OR
7. **Drag and drop** your updated `LeafyPop` folder again

---

### If You Used **GitHub Pages**:

**If you uploaded via GitHub:**
1. Go to your GitHub repository
2. Upload your updated files (replace the old ones)
3. Commit the changes
4. GitHub Pages will automatically update in 1-2 minutes

**If you uploaded directly:**
- Just re-upload all files from your `LeafyPop` folder
- Replace the old files with new ones

---

### If You Used **Cloudflare Pages**:

1. Go to [https://dash.cloudflare.com](https://dash.cloudflare.com)
2. Select **Pages**
3. Click on your project
4. Go to **Deployments**
5. Click **"Create deployment"**
6. Upload your updated `LeafyPop` folder
