What Are Microgreens?

Have you ever seen a tiny plant bursting with life? That’s a Microgreen — the young, baby version of vegetables like broccoli, radish, and beetroot.

They are harvested at just 7-14 days old, right when their first true leaves appear. Despite their small size, they pack a massive punch!

How It Grows Matters

Unlike traditional farming, our process is strictly controlled for purity and safety.

🌱

1. Pure Start

We start with non-GMO seeds in clean, soil-less trays. No dirt residues, no pests.

💧

2. Controlled Growth

Grown securely indoors with filtered water, perfect light, and strict airflow control.

✂️

3. Peak Harvest

Harvested exactly at day 10-14 when nutrients are at their absolute peak level.
